# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

High school students at SMA Regina Pacis Surakarta who are scoping out the club before joining. They are curious, often have no prior technical experience, and are deciding whether URSU Technology is worth their time. Secondary audience: current members who use the site for events, division info, and resources.

## Product Purpose

URSU Technology is a student-run technology club with three divisions: Metaverse & Web Design, Robotics & Embedded Systems, and 3D Printing & Rapid Prototyping. This website recruits new members, showcases division work, and communicates upcoming events. Success looks like a prospective student reading the site and feeling confident enough to fill out the join form.

## Brand Personality

Accessible, energetic, welcoming. The club is serious about building real things but never intimidating about it. Voice is direct and enthusiastic without being corporate or hype-driven.

## Anti-references

- Generic school club site: boring templates, clip-art icons, flat colors, zero personality.
- Corporate tech company: cold, formal, navy-and-white, no warmth or approachability.

## Design Principles

1. **Lower the barrier** — Every touchpoint should feel like an invitation. Beginners should never feel like outsiders.
2. **Prove, don't promise** — Show actual division work, real member counts, shipped projects. Demonstrations beat marketing copy.
3. **Technical credibility, human warmth** — The aesthetic earns trust through precision and craft, but never at the expense of approachability.
4. **Personality over polish** — Club character matters more than corporate perfection. Idiosyncratic and genuine beats smooth and forgettable.
5. **One club, three distinct voices** — Each division has its own color and personality; the site should feel cohesive but let each division breathe.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA as the target. Good contrast ratios (4.5:1 for normal text), keyboard navigable, screen reader friendly. Reduced motion support is a nice-to-have for the animation-heavy hero.
